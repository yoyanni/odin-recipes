Coleslaw

Description

This sweet, creamy coleslaw recipe tastes like the coleslaw served at popular fried chicken or fish restaurants. It's excellent with burgers or on top of BBQ pork sandwiches, too!

Recipe by Yanni Alevras

Ingredient

  • 1 (16 ounce) bag coleslaw mix
  • 2 tablespoons diced onion
  • 2/3 cup creamy salad dressing
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon poppy seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Combine coleslaw mix and onion in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk together salad dressing, sugar, vegetable oil, vinegar, poppy seeds, and salt in a medium bowl; blend thoroughly. Pour dressing over coleslaw mixture and toss to coat.
  3. Chill for at least 2 hours before serving.
